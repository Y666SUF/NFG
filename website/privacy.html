NFG Crash Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 May 2026

Introduction

NFG Crash (“the App”) is a companion application for a TikTok LIVE “crash” style game operated by the stream host. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the App.

The App uses virtual points for entertainment and leaderboard competition. There is no cash-out, no withdrawals, and no real-money gambling within the App.

Who we are

The data controller for the App is the operator of the NFG Crash live game service (the “Service”), contactable at privacy@y666suf.com.

The App connects to game servers operated for the Service, including hosts under y666suf.com.

Information we collect

  • Account / identity: TikTok username and display name after you verify ownership by commenting on the host’s live stream; a session token stored securely on your device.
  • Device: a random device identifier sent with API requests; iOS version and app version for support.
  • Gameplay: virtual point balance, bets, cash-out targets, chat messages you send in App chat, and related game state stored on the Service.
  • Advertising: if you watch optional rewarded ads, Google AdMob may collect device identifiers, ad interaction data, and (if you allow it on iOS) data used for personalized advertising.
  • Technical logs: IP address and request timestamps on our servers for security and reliability (e.g. via Cloudflare).

How we use information

We use information to operate the game, verify your TikTok account, show leaderboards and chat, deliver optional rewarded ads, and keep the Service secure. We do not sell your personal information.

Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

Where applicable law requires a legal basis, we rely on performance of the service you request, legitimate interests in operating a safe live game companion, and your consent where required (for example, optional ad tracking via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency).

Sharing with third parties

We may share information with our game servers and hosting providers (including Cloudflare), Google (AdMob) when you view ads, and TikTok when you use the live stream. App chat messages may be visible to other players during the same session. We may disclose information if required by law.

Google privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Retention

We keep account and gameplay data while your account is active. Server logs are retained for a limited period. Contact us to request deletion of server-side account data where applicable.

Security

We use HTTPS and authenticated sessions. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We recommend users aged 17+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. Contact privacy@y666suf.com. On iOS you can limit ad tracking under Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

International transfers

Data may be processed in the United Kingdom and other countries where our servers or providers operate.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact
